About Us

We CARe About Your Recovery

Our vision at CARe is to be Comprehensive, Advanced, Responsive, and Excellent in providing rehabilitation and support. We strive to offer rehabilitation services that address the physical, emotional, and cognitive needs of our patients.

By staying at the forefront of advancements in rehabilitation techniques and technology, we ensure that our interventions are effective and evidence-based. All our Physiotherapists and Occupational Therapists are registered with the Allied Health Professional Council (AHPC) in Singapore.
